My Local Trips and Furthest Travels

I love traveling and I believe that traveling, whether in your own state or not, is crucial for not only mental health, but experience and knowledge.

"The more you do, the more you CAN do." Kudos to my mother for instilling this in my life.

It seems simple.. and it is. The main idea of this quote is to always do as much as you can, whenever you can. The more you experience, the more skills you will add to your toolkit in life, and the more insight you will have on thoughts other than your own. Whenever I feel overwhelmed with tasks, plans, and activities, I remind myself I am only adding value to my life, and allowing myself to know more, and DO more!

What's the purpose of this blog?

This blog is just for my friends, family and myself to "keep up." Nothing crazy! Can't wait to share my thoughts and travels!
